Build Note Phase 1

Why Nova is being built as a project command center, not just another chatbot.

Most AI tools are built around a single prompt. Nova is being built around the project. That difference matters when the work takes days, files, sessions, decisions, fixes, deployments, and repeated context.

The goal is simple: keep the mission together. A useful assistant should remember what was already decided, understand the active work, restore older sessions, handle files, support voice, track usage, and help push the next piece forward.

Nova’s strongest lane right now is the desktop workspace: fast, stable, and practical. Around that foundation, the public site, billing dashboard, memory system, and future model-tier controls can grow into a real product.
