How credits work

Simple account rules before real payments.

Nova is keeping billing understandable first: credits show available usage, monthly allowance shows the planned budget, and model tiers stay marked as planned until backend enforcement is complete.
01

Credits are the usage budget

Credits represent how much Nova usage the account has available. The current page reads that balance from the local billing ledger.

02

Plans explain access level

The plan label explains account mode today. Later, plans can control monthly credits, feature access, and model availability.

03

Model tiers stay backend-owned

Mini, Standard, and Pro should eventually be enforced by backend allowlists and credit costs, not just frontend buttons.
