Privacy overview

Project context is part of the product.

Nova is built around ongoing project work. That means conversations, sessions, saved context, files, and usage information may be handled so the workspace can continue a project instead of treating every conversation as brand new.
Contact Nova Read Terms

01 ? Account and sessions

Workspace information may be stored.

Nova may store account identifiers, session information, conversation history, titles, timestamps, and related metadata needed to operate and restore the workspace.

02 ? Project context and memory

Useful context may continue across conversations.

Nova may retain project facts, preferences, decisions, checkpoints, and other saved context used to help continue longer-running work.

03 ? Files and attachments

Uploaded material may be processed for the task.

Files and attachments may be stored or processed so Nova can upload, preview, summarize, analyze, or use them as project context. Do not upload material you are not comfortable providing to the service.

04 ? Usage and account status

Nova may keep operational usage information.

Usage counts, credit balances, plan labels, feature access, request metadata, and similar account information may be recorded to operate and understand the service.

05 ? Service providers

Some information may be processed by infrastructure providers.

Nova may rely on third-party services for AI processing, hosting, storage, security, or related infrastructure. Information needed to provide a feature may be sent to the services involved in delivering that feature.

06 ? Retention and changes

Privacy handling may change as the service changes.

Retention behavior, account controls, integrations, and service operations may change as Nova develops. This page may be updated to reflect material changes in how the product handles information.

Early-access clarity

Use Nova for project work, but choose what you provide deliberately.

Nova is still developing its broader account, retention, and service controls. Avoid entering passwords, private keys, payment card numbers, or other secrets into conversations or uploads. For questions about project information or account handling, contact Nova.
