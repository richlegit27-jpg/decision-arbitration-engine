Privacy overview

Built for project work that should stay under your control.

Nova is an early-access AI workspace. This page explains the intended privacy posture for project sessions, uploads, memory, billing credits, and product usage while the public product is still being polished.
Contact Nova View Terms

Project sessions

Chats may be saved.

Nova is designed around long-running work. Sessions, messages, titles, and metadata may be stored so the workspace can restore context later.

Uploads

Files support the workspace.

Uploaded files may be processed to summarize, analyze, preview, or attach them to a project. Avoid uploading anything you do not want used inside Nova.

Memory

Context improves continuity.

Nova may use project memory and saved state to keep track of decisions, bugs, sessions, plans, and next actions across a longer build cycle.

Billing

Credits may be tracked.

The billing dashboard may track plan status, usage estimates, model usage, and account-level credit information as billing features mature.

Data selling

No data resale product.

Nova is being built as a private project command center, not a marketplace for user conversations or uploaded files.

Early access

Expect changes.

Privacy details, retention behavior, integrations, and account controls may change as the product moves toward public launch.

Important note

This is a plain-language product privacy page, not a final legal policy.

Before public launch, this page should be reviewed and upgraded with formal legal language matching the final hosting stack, analytics, billing provider, AI provider, and retention settings.
