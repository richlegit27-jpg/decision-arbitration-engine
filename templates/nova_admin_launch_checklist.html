Owner launch readiness

Public-site launch checklist

Admin-only truth sheet for what is live, what is planned, and what still blocks real users, payments, and wider promotion.

Live

Public site

  • Root homepage renders the polished public home.
  • Public navigation and footer are consistent.
  • FAQ, billing, contact, roadmap, blog, privacy, and terms render.
  • Canonical SEO cleanup is locked.
Live

Lead capture

  • Contact and Early Access submissions are saved.
  • Admin lead review is available.
  • Lead CSV export passes release checks.
  • Owner-only admin pills stay hidden publicly.
Live

Release checks

  • Public smoke checks run.
  • Admin release check passes.
  • Railway release check passes.
  • Ship check guards accidental mobile changes.
Planned

Payments

  • Stripe checkout is still planned.
  • Paid plan activation is still planned.
  • Production payment collection is not live yet.
  • Billing copy explains the planned payment state.
Planned

Model tiers

  • User-selectable models are planned.
  • Model-based credit costs are planned.
  • Backend model allowlist should come before paid launch.
  • Usage ledger should stay server-side and auditable.
Before paid launch

Must finish

  • Stripe live-mode checkout and webhook verification.
  • Account plan state tied to payment state.
  • Usage limits and credit enforcement tested end-to-end.
  • Final privacy/terms review before taking money.
Operating rule

Launch rule

  • Early Access can collect interest without payment collection.
  • Do not claim paid launch until Stripe, plans, usage, and account state are wired together.
  • Do not touch mobile/app internals during public-site polish unless explicitly starting that phase.
